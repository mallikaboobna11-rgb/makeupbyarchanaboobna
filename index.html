The Artist

A quiet kind of luxury

Eighteen years shaping fabric into feeling, now three years shaping faces into their most luminous selves.

Archana Boobna is a bridal and luxury makeup artist based in Patna, known for looks that enhance rather than overpower. Her signature is natural, classy and elegant — makeup that a bride's own mother recognises as her daughter, only turned up to her most beautiful setting.

Before makeup, Archana spent eighteen years as a fashion designer and boutique owner — a craft she still practises, and one that quietly informs every face she finishes. She understands how colour sits against skin, how a drape frames a collarbone, how an earring catches the light.

What began as a personal passion raised alongside a family has become a full profession, taken seriously: certified by the London School of Makeup, trained under Amrit Kaur in Delhi and Ritika Kadam in Mumbai, and sharpened through ongoing masterclasses and international workshops.

Her brides leave the chair feeling confident, comfortable, and — most of all — like themselves.
